Does unemployment cause an unhealthy lifestyle? This visualization tries to give a data-based answer to that question. In the map we see the unemployment rates in Europa. Hover over the countries to see the actual numbers. Click on one to see how the unemployment develops in comparison with tobacco and alcohol consumption over the years.
